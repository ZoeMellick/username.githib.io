About

I am a PhD candidate and teacher in Fashion at QUT in Brisbane. My PhD research looks at how sustainability is valued in textile and apparel supply chains. Prior to starting my PhD, I worked for over a decade in retail with Australian fashion brands. I am passionate about contributing to the space of sustainable fashion and my research is motivated by real-world challenges currently facing the global fashion industry.
